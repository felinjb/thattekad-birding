Welcome to Thattekad

Nestled between the branches of the Periyar River, Thattekad is the richest bird habitat in peninsular India. Established in 1983 by the renowned ornithologist Dr. Salim Ali, this 25-square-kilometer tropical evergreen forest is a haven for rare and endemic species.

Whether you are a professional wildlife photographer looking for the perfect shot, or a nature enthusiast wanting to experience the dawn chorus, our expert trackers provide intimate, small-group expeditions tailored to your goals.

Expert Trackers

Our indigenous guides know the exact nesting spots and calls of Thattekad's rarest endemics.
