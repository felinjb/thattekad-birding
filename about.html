THE INDIGENOUS TRACKERS

We are a highly specialized team of local conservationists and indigenous trackers who have spent our entire lives navigating the complex canopy of the Salim Ali Bird Sanctuary. Our deep understanding of the forest allows us to identify calls that others miss, and locate rare endemics without disturbing the delicate ecosystem.

We believe that true wildlife photography requires patience, respect for the subject, and an intimate familiarity with the terrain.
