ABOUT US

Meet the local experts of Salim Ali Bird Sanctuary

WHY BIRD WITH US?

Thattekad Birding is more than just a tour. We are a team of local experts born and raised at the edge of the Salim Ali Bird Sanctuary. We don't just find birds; we understand their behavior, calls, and seasonal patterns.

  • Expertise in Sri Lanka Frogmouth & Malabar Trogon
  • Customized trails for hardcore Photographers
  • Support for indigenous local trackers

OUR IMPACT

By birding with us, you are directly supporting the local indigenous trackers and contributing to the conservation of the Western Ghats ecosystem.
