Know Before You Go

Cancellations & Weather

Birding relies on nature. We operate rain or shine. Full refunds are provided for cancellations made 48 hours in advance. If severe weather (e.g., heavy monsoon flooding) forces us to cancel, you will be fully refunded or rescheduled.

What to Wear

Please wear dull-colored, breathable clothing (greens, browns, greys) to blend into the forest. Bright colors startle the birds. Closed-toe trekking shoes and long trousers are mandatory.

Leech Protection

During damp months, leeches are a natural part of the ecosystem. We provide organic salt/tobacco powder repellents, but leech socks are highly recommended if you are visiting right after the monsoon.

Gear & Ethics

Bring your own binoculars and camera gear. We practice strict ethical birding: no playback calls to stress the birds, and no flash photography for nocturnal species.
